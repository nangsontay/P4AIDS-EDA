Core Methodology

The EDA-Driven Modeling Paradigm

Rather than arbitrarily applying algorithms out-of-the-box, every decision in this report is traceable back to a specific finding in Assignment 1. Statistical anomalies became preprocessing rules. Semantic overlaps dictated architectural choice. Spatial imbalances selected the evaluation metric.

◆ Pipeline philosophy

EDA findings are not just visualizations — they are engineering specifications. Each anomaly discovered in Assignment 1 maps directly to a code-level decision in Assignment 2.

Modality 01 of 03

Tabular — Banking Marketing

Binary classification: predict whether a customer will subscribe to a term deposit (y), while neutralizing severe class imbalance, non-linear demographic behaviors, and — most critically — mitigating fatal predictive data leakage.

BINARY CLASSIFICATION · 88.3% / 11.7% IMBALANCE

Predicting Term Deposit Subscription

A scikit-learn ColumnTransformer pipeline driven by EDA findings, paired with a structured three-model ablation study to expose the danger of post-facto data leakage.

Structured Data
EDA findings → Feature engineering
!
The Data Leakage Imperative Critical
Boxplot analysis revealed a massive divergence in duration: the 'no' class averaged 221 seconds, while the 'yes' class averaged 537 seconds. But business logic dictates duration is a post-facto variable — it is only recorded after the call concludes. To build a deployable model that scores leads before calling, duration was strictly eradicated from X_clean to prevent "time-travel" leakage.
σ
Outlier Neutralization via RobustScaler
KDE and Boxplot analyses revealed extreme right-skewed, heavy-tail distributions in balance and age. The balance feature contained both severe debt (negatives) and extreme wealth outliers. Standard MinMaxScaler or StandardScaler would collapse 99% of the data into a microscopic manifold. RobustScaler, built on the IQR, was used to scale continuous variables — inherently immune to extremes.
⊘
"Missing Data" as a Behavioral Signal
High percentages of unknown values were found in poutcome (81.75%) and contact (28.80%). Instead of standard imputation (which destroys data authenticity), unknown was preserved as an independent category and one-hot encoded. EDA proved that "lack of prior banking history" is itself a highly predictive behavioral signal.
∼
Non-Linear Binning — The Customer Fatigue Curve
EDA line charts identified "Customer Fatigue": conversion rates peaked at the first contact and plummeted after 4 contacts. To prevent tree-based models from overfitting to the noisy long tail (>10 calls), campaign was discretized into ordinal bins ('1', '2-3', '4-5', '>5'). Because >81% of pdays was -1 (no prior contact), an explicit binary indicator is_previously_contacted was engineered so models map this threshold instantly.

Modality 02 of 03

Text — AG News Topic Classification

Contextual 4-class categorization of short-form news articles (World, Sports, Business, Sci/Tech). A direct comparison of sparse lexical (TF-IDF) vs. dense contextual (DistilBERT) paradigms — designed to validate the t-SNE "Semantic Blind Spot" hypothesis from Assignment 1.

4-CLASS · BALANCED 25%/CLASS

Sparse Lexical vs. Dense Contextual

Targeted noise eradication, n-gram parameterization, and tensor optimization driven by EDA-derived sequence-length statistics — culminating in an empirical proof of polysemy resolution.

NLP
NLP diagnostics → Lexical representation
⊕
Contextual Maximization
Because journalistic intent was fragmented across titles and subheaders, the title and description fields were concatenated into a unified text string, providing maximum semantic density per sample.
⊘
Targeted Noise Eradication via Custom Stopwords
Unigram frequency analysis revealed journalistic boilerplate words ('reuters', 'ap', 'said', 'quot', 'new', 'year') appeared uniformly across all four classes. A custom_sw list was injected into TfidfVectorizer to filter these high-frequency noise vectors — preventing them from blurring hyper-plane decision boundaries.
N
N-gram Parameterization
EDA showed unigrams were ambiguous, whereas Bigrams ('wall street', 'oil prices', 'champions league') sharply delineated topics. The feature extractor was strictly parameterized to ngram_range=(1, 2).
τ
Deep-Learning Tensor Optimization
Sequence-length KDE distributions proved 95% of texts contained ≤ 53 words. This precise statistical threshold was hardcoded into the HuggingFace AutoTokenizer as max_length=53 for padding and truncation — retaining >95% of the semantic payload while exponentially reducing memory and FLOPs during attention-matrix calculations.
Methods — sparse vs. dense architectures
Baseline
TF-IDF + Logistic Regression
A sparse, frequency-based lexical model capped at 10,000 dimensions. Exceptionally fast and interpretable, but treats words as isolated, orderless tokens (Bag-of-Words paradigm).
Transfer Learning
DistilBERT (distilbert-base-uncased)
A dense, bidirectional contextual Transformer. Multi-Head Self-Attention contextually disambiguates words — distinguishing "Apple" the stock (Business) from "Apple" the developer (Sci/Tech).
◆ Why DistilBERT? — The t-SNE Hypothesis

Assignment 1's t-SNE clustering revealed a massive geometric overlap between Business and Sci/Tech because they share vocabulary ("Apple", "Microsoft", "company", "stocks"). DistilBERT was deployed because Self-Attention contextually disambiguates polysemous tokens based on surrounding context — a capability fundamentally absent in TF-IDF.

Modality 03 of 03

Image — ADE20K Semantic Segmentation

Pixel-level dense prediction across an unconstrained visual dataset with 150 unique semantic classes. A meticulously engineered PyTorch pipeline survives extreme aspect-ratio variance, MPS hardware quirks, and a brutal "Pixel vs. Instance Paradox" — culminating in a Vision-Transformer solution.

SEMANTIC SEGMENTATION · 150 CLASSES

SegFormer (ViT) on ADE20K

Aspect-ratio-aware preprocessing, MPS contiguity patches, and a hierarchical Vision Transformer architecture chosen specifically to resolve the global-receptive-field and micro-object-destruction bottlenecks identified in EDA.

Vision
Spatial & statistical EDA → Pipeline engineering
▭
Geometric Preservation — Aspect Ratio Variance
EDA scatter plots revealed extreme variations in image aspect ratios (1:1 squares to 16:9 widescreen). Standard direct resizing to 512×512 would distort circular wheels into ovals, destroying spatial priors. SegformerImageProcessor was configured with dynamic longest-edge resizing + zero-padding (do_resize=True) — maintaining aspect ratios while fitting batch tensor shape.
⚙
Hardware & Tensor Integrity Patches MPS
Multi-dimensional data handling on Apple Silicon (MPS) creates non-contiguous memory allocations during stride operations. A custom contiguous_data_collator was written to enforce .contiguous() memory blocks; the model's decode_head_forward was dynamically monkey-patched to prevent autograd crashes during sequence-to-grid reshaping.
Architecture — why traditional ML fundamentally fails here

Applying Random Forests or SVMs to ADE20K would mean extracting flattened raw pixels or relying on hand-crafted SIFT/HOG features. Two specific EDA findings make this approach mathematically untenable:

Failure Mode 01
Lack of Global Receptive Field
EDA co-occurrence heatmaps proved objects follow strict spatial logic ("cars" with "roads"; "ceiling" never touches "ground"). Traditional ML and basic CNNs see only local textures — they cannot perceive the whole image to infer this logic.
Failure Mode 02
Destruction of Micro-Objects
EDA proved a terrifying imbalance: 59.1% of object instances are "micro-objects" (<2% of image pixels). Max-Pooling layers in deep CNNs physically crush spatial dimensions — deleting "doorknobs" and "birds" before the classifier ever sees them.
◆ The Transfer-Learning Solution — SegFormer (nvidia/mit-b0)

SegFormer is a hierarchical Vision Transformer pre-trained on ImageNet. It maps directly onto the EDA bottlenecks:

01 →
Self-Attention maps to Co-occurrence. Every pixel attends to every other pixel from layer 1, inherently learning the global co-occurrence logic of scenes.
02 →
All-MLP Decode Head maps to Scale Variance. Bypasses traditional pooling destruction; fuses high-resolution shallow layers (rescuing <2% micro-object boundaries) with deep semantic features.
Evaluation — rejecting Pixel Accuracy for mIoU

EDA Pareto analysis proved a catastrophic long-tail: classes like "wall" and "sky" dominate pixel count, while 87% of classes are exceptionally rare. A model could score 80% Pixel Accuracy by simply painting the top half of the screen blue (sky) and the bottom half grey (wall). Mean Intersection over Union (mIoU) was enforced as the sole metric.

Synthesis

Cross-Modal Key Takeaways

Three modalities, three architectures, one consistent thesis: rigorous EDA is not a preliminary formality — it is the engineering specification for every downstream decision.

Modality Core EDA Anomaly Engineering Response Key Metric

☕ The unifying principle

Across all three modalities, the same lesson surfaces: the metric you choose, the feature you drop, and the architecture you pick must all answer to a finding from EDA. Accuracy is rejected for AUC when classes are imbalanced. TF-IDF is rejected for Transformers when polysemy is structural. Pixel Accuracy is rejected for mIoU when class distributions follow Pareto. Every choice traceable. Every decision defensible.
